Employee Access Standard Operating Procedure

Purpose:
This SOP explains how employees receive, review, and lose access to internal systems.

Access request workflow:
1. Employee submits an access request.
2. Manager approves the business need.
3. IT admin assigns the minimum required permissions.
4. Security team logs the access grant.
5. Access is reviewed every 90 days.

Role table:
Role | Access Level | Review Frequency
Intern | Limited project access | 30 days
Engineer | Repository and staging tools | 90 days
Manager | Team dashboards and approvals | 90 days
Admin | Production and identity systems | 30 days

Offboarding:
All system access must be revoked within 24 hours of employee exit.

Sensitivity:
This document is confidential because it describes internal access controls.
