Internal AI Usage Policy

This document defines safe and responsible usage of AI systems inside the organization.

Employees may use approved AI tools for summarization, drafting, coding assistance, research support, and workflow automation.

Sensitive data handling:
Employees must not upload confidential client data, passwords, API keys, private customer records, financial account details, or health information into public AI tools.

Employee access:
Only authorized employees may access internal AI assistants. Access must be reviewed every quarter by the security team.

Responsible automation:
AI-generated outputs must be reviewed by a human before being used in customer communication, legal documents, financial reports, or production systems.

Sensitivity level:
This document is classified as internal because it contains operational security guidance and employee access rules.
